Department of Wildlife Conservation

The Department of Wildlife Conservation in Sri Lanka is a government agency responsible for the conservation and management of the country's diverse and rich wildlife. The department operates under the Ministry of Environment, fulfilling its mandate to protect and preserve the natural habitats and biodiversity of Sri Lanka.
